Miwa

Miwa is a cosplayer well-known for her Hoshimachi Suisei cosplay. Her cosplay name came from a Jujutsu Kaisen Character "Miwa" which she cosplayed back in Cosplay Carnival 2022. She debuted last 2022 and is currently rising in popularity due to her Hololive Cosplays. She apparently dislikes vegetables.
